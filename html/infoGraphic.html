Introduction
This app allows users to see where investments may achieve the most co-benefits for people and nature. Users can identify watersheds that meet user-defined criteria related to biodiversity conservation, climate change mitigation, and human health and well-being, as an aid in prioritizing investment and action. Users can also select individual watersheds and view more detailed information about what co-benefits may, or may not, be enhanced by investments in that watershed.
Climate Change Mitigation/Carbon
Activities like reforestation, forest protection, and agricultural BMPs can mitigate climate change by avoiding carbon dioxide emissions through actions that safeguard carbon stored in soil and plants and sequestering carbon through plant growth.
